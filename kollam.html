Kollam also known as Quilon or Desinganadu, Kollam is an old sea port town situated between Arabian Coast and the Ashtamudi Lake in Kerala. This place has been a flourishing trading port for centuries and it is the centre of cashew processing and the hub of fisheries industry. The surrounding region of the place produces pepper, coir, cashews and other agricultural products. Kollam is one of the oldest ports on the Malabar Coast, which draws the attraction of international countries.
